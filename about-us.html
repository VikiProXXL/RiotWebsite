ABOUT THEM

Riot Games, Inc. is an American video game developer, publisher, and esports tournament organizer based in Los Angeles, California.

Originaly made a game called League of Legend and after its success made more popular games

They are not only popular for their games but also for their movie series, e-sports events and even music

Riot Games is acquired by Chinese conglomerate Tencent.
